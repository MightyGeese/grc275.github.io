Why Daniel likes Guitars:

When he was in high school, Daniel's favorite band was Led Zeppelin. He enjoyed the creative variety of songs that were recorded in the band's 12 year history. Soon Daniel had collected all of their studio albums on CD, and appreciated the sound/style of each one. Jimmy Page's guitar skills were impressive, and Daniel learned to play many of the band's songs on guitar. Eventually, he had saved up some money, and with the help of his parents - got a Gibson Les Paul guitar, the same as Page used. This guitar is still part of Daniel's collection and it is a very well-crafted instrument of high quality.
